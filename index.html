Virtual University (VU) is Pakistan’s first fully online public sector university operating under modern digital infrastructure. It provides flexible, affordable, and technology-based education to students across Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad (FSD Pakistan), Multan, Bahawalpur (BWP Pakistan), and other cities.

Introduction to Virtual University of Pakistan

Established in 2002, the Virtual University of Pakistan was created to promote distance learning through Information and Communication Technologies (ICT). Unlike traditional universities, VU delivers lectures through recorded video sessions, digital handouts, and an advanced Learning Management System (LMS).

The university is recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan. This means VU degrees are valid for government jobs, private sector employment, and further studies like MS, M.Phil, and PhD.
