Virtual University of Pakistan 2026
Admission, Fee Structure, LMS & Programs Guide

Introduction:

Looking for complete information about Virtual University of Pakistan in 2026? This guide covers VU admission 2026, fee structure, VULMS login process, programs offered, exams, scholarships, and student support services.
Virtual University of Pakistan is one of the leading public sector universities offering online and distance learning programs across Pakistan and internationally. Established in 2002, VU provides affordable, flexible, and technology-driven education.

VU Admission 2026 Complete Process

Virtual University offers admissions multiple times a year for undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs.

Admission Requirements:

  • Minimum qualification as per program criteria
  • Online application submission
  • Fee payment confirmation
  • Document verification

Students can apply online through the official admission portal.

VU LMS Login Student Portal Guide

The Virtual University Learning Management System (VULMS) is the main platform for students.
Through VULMS, students can:

  • Submit assignments
  • Attempt quizzes
  • Participate in GDB
  • Download handouts
  • Check results
  • Track academic progress

Students can access VULMS using their student ID and password via the official portal.

Virtual University Fee Structure 2026

VU offers affordable education compared to many traditional universities.
Students can:

  • Pay through bank or online payment options
  • Apply for scholarships

Scholarships Available:

  • Financial assistance programs

VU Exam System

Virtual University follows a structured evaluation system:

  1. Assignments
  2. Quizzes
  3. Graded Discussion Board (GDB)
  4. Midterm Exams
  5. Final Term Exams

Exams are conducted in designated centers across Pakistan.
